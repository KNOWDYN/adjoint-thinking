# Security and Privacy Notes

Prompt packs are not secure data-processing systems. Do not upload secrets or regulated data without understanding platform controls.

## Sensitive data

Do not upload secrets, API keys, passwords, client confidential material, patient data, legal secrets, regulated data, or private financial records unless you understand your platform account controls.

## Known risks

- Prompt injection inside uploaded files.
- Unsupported factual claims.
- Overreliance on model fluency.
- Commercial misuse beyond the license.
