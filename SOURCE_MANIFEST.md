## Source-grounded method spine

The pack preserves these source-supported principles:

- The package is not a prompt handbook.
- Use machines without surrendering attention, judgment, originality, verification, synthesis, invention, or the human verdict.
- Preserve first contact before machine framing.
- Separate mine, shareable, and dangerous offloads.
- Preserve provenance, verification status, and human judgment.
- Treat machine output as status-uncertain material until checked.
- Use reasoning paths without borrowing verdicts.
- Use divergence as provisional material.
- Verify before public or consequential use.
- Synthesize through material, status, and placement before prose.
- Transform outputs under constraint and reality contact.
- Preserve sovereignty and the final human verdict.
