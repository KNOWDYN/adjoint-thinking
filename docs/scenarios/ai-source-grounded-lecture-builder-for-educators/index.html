Educator scenario

AI Source-Grounded Lecture Builder for Educators

Turn source material into a lecture without allowing AI to replace evidence, structure, or teaching judgment.

What can go wrong

A smooth lecture outline can make weak evidence and strong evidence appear equal.

Situation

The educator has sources, examples, and a lesson goal, but the lecture needs a defensible structure and clear boundaries.
