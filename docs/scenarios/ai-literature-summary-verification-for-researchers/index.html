Researcher scenario

AI Literature Summary Verification for Researchers

Use an Adjoint Thinking environment to check AI-assisted literature summaries before they enter a paper, review, lecture, or research note.

What can go wrong

Source distortion and overgeneralization can weaken a paper before the author notices.

Situation

A paper summary sounds coherent, but the researcher does not know whether it preserved the method, population, limitations, and claim boundary.

Guided synapse

PressureA paper summary sounds coherent, but the researcher does not know whether it preserved the method, population, limitations, and claim boundary.

Use this prompt

Route this AI-assisted literature summary through the Verification Receipt. Extract the claims, source paths, boundaries, contradictions, and trust label before I use it in my research draft.
