Student scenario

AI Learning Without Outsourcing Understanding for Students

Use AI as a learning aid while preserving first contact, effort, and human understanding.

What can go wrong

AI can make a student feel informed before understanding has formed.

Situation

The student wants help, but asking too early may replace the struggle that would build understanding.
